Welcome to IQ_Spectrum

IQ Spectrum Society is a student-driven community dedicated to empowering learners through Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Programming, Technology, Innovation, and Professional Skill Development.

We organize internships, workshops, technical events, coding competitions, and career development programs to help students build practical skills and prepare for the future.
